Once you use these sentences before your question, take into account the importance of changing the word order of the information that is next, for instance: Could you tell me what is the alternate airport? : That is wrong....You’d better say: Could you tell me what the alternate airport is? : Then, the person you are interacting with is going to easily understand your intention and he or she is going to answer you faster and clear.
